Site Plan

Site Purpose

What kind of rare item would you like to own? If you owned it would you display it? Would you ever consider displaying it on a site where you could share history, notable facts etc and where others could comment and discuss the information about your rare item? Imagine if you wanted to expand your collection of rare items. This same site will offer options to buy, sell or trade items with the ability to browse collections by type and keywords. This site will create an amazing community with opportunities to share the love of collecting as well as an opportunity to expand and progress your collection.

Content

Home Page

Welcome to on display where collectors of all genres come to share their collections. From stamps to cars you can peruse and learn about what makes these collections unique and special to each collector.

Share with others your own collection and give feedback to inquiries about your pieces. Create groups with those that have similar interests to expand your knowledge as well as your collection.

Buying and Selling

While enjoying others displays you can also expand upon your own collection by taking note of others' items that are up for purchase and/or for trade.

On the other side of that same coin (pun intended for you numismatists) you can also put items in your own collection up for sale or set parameters for a trade. Let your collecting experience expand while enjoying others amazing collections.

About Us

We started this journey with an idea to share our love of collecting with others. We wanted to find a way to connect with collectors of all types to share our displays and create a like minded community.

We love sharing our interest with others but more than that we love seeing a person's eyes light up in excitement when sharing their passion. We hope to see many eyes light up in excitement when sharing and growing within our on display community.
