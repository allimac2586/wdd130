Dry Oar Rafting Co.

Whitewater

If you are up for a thrill, then the whitewater package is for you. Included in the package is a life jacket, helmet, transportation to and from the river, and last but not least 50 minutes of exhilerating adventure. You can rent a wetsuit at an additional cost. - $75

Scenic

If you are looking to enjoy the beauty and peace of the river the scenic package is for you. Included is a life jacket, transportation to and from the river, and a wonderful 50 minutes of floating down the river surrounded by beatiful views. - $65

Whitewater & Scenic Package

If you want the best of both worlds then our combined package is for you. Included in the package is a life jacket, helmet for the whitewater rafting portion, transportation to and from the river, 50 minutes of whitewater rafting and 50 minutes of scenic rafting. You can schedule these rafting trips on the same day or within one week of each other. The whhitewater rafting and the scenic rafting are on different parts of the river so you will have to be transported back to our main hub before heading to your other trip. As well as we have different guides for each trip. - $125
